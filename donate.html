Support the Windhoek Deanery Choir

Your generous contributions empower the Windhoek Deanery Choir to continue our mission of sharing sacred music, fostering community, and enriching lives through our performances. Every donation, big or small, makes a significant difference.

How Your Support Helps:

  • Purchasing new sheet music and musical arrangements.
  • Covering transportation costs for choir members to rehearsals and venues.
  • Maintaining and upgrading sound equipment.
  • Procuring performance attire and cultural outfits.
  • Supporting workshops and vocal training for choir development.
  • Funding venue rentals and event production for concerts like "Sing Without Borders."
