About Windhoek Deanery Choir

The Windhoek Deanery Choir (WDC) is a vibrant and dedicated group of singers passionate about using music to glorify God and foster community. Established with the vision of bringing together diverse voices from across the deanery, we strive for excellence in our performances while upholding the spirit of worship and fellowship.

Our choir is composed of individuals from various backgrounds, united by their love for choral music and their commitment to spreading messages of hope, joy, and faith through song. We believe that music transcends boundaries and has the power to connect people from all walks of life.

Through regular rehearsals, prayer, and shared experiences, we not only hone our musical skills but also build strong bonds of friendship and mutual support. The WDC is more than just a choir; it is a family dedicated to ministering through music.

Our Mission

To inspire, uplift, and unite through the power of sacred music, fostering a deeper connection to faith and community within the Windhoek Deanery and beyond.

Our Vision

To be a beacon of musical excellence and spiritual enrichment, celebrating our cultural diversity and sharing God's love through harmonious praise.
