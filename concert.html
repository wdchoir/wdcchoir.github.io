About Windhoek Deanery Choir

Our Mission

To bring together Catholic voices across Windhoek's parishes in a joyful expression of faith, unity, and cultural celebration through sacred music.

"One voice. One spirit. One choir. Many cultures — singing for Christ."

Who We Are

The Windhoek Deanery Choir is a multigenerational Catholic choir collective serving under the Archdiocese of Windhoek. From liturgical service to community outreach, we are committed to sharing the Gospel through music that uplifts and inspires.

Musical Identity

  • Sacred hymns, contemporary gospel, and ethnic praise
  • Repertoire includes songs in English, Oshiwambo, Khoekhoegowab, German, Afrikaans, Otjiherero, and more
  • Vocal unity that reflects Namibia's diversity

Sing Without Borders 2025

Overview

"Sing Without Borders" is more than a concert — it's a celebration of faith and heritage. Two unique evenings filled with passion, praise, and cultural expression. Expect traditional drums, choir harmonies, graceful movement, and visual storytelling.
