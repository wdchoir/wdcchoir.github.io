Voices United: Our Choir Sections

At the heart of the Windhoek Deanery Choir are our dedicated and diverse members. This page celebrates the unique contributions of each voice section that comes together to create our harmonious sound.

Soprano

The highest vocal range, sopranos often carry the melody, bringing brightness and clarity to our choir's sound. Their voices soar, guiding the audience through the spiritual journey of our music.

Alto

Providing the crucial harmonic foundation for the higher voices, the altos contribute richness and depth, adding warmth and fullness to our choir's overall blend.

Tenor

The tenors bring a unique blend of power and lyricism, often bridging the gap between male and female voices. Their harmonies add excitement and drive to our arrangements.

Bass

The foundation of our choir's sound, the basses provide the deep, resonant notes that give our music its grounded and rich quality. Their strong presence is indispensable.

Together, these diverse voices form the Windhoek Deanery Choir – a family united in song and faith. Each member brings a unique quality, contributing to our rich and diverse sound and our shared mission of praise.
